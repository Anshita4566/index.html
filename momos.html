Veg Momos Recipe

1. Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water (for kneading)

2. Prepare the Dough

Mix all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt. Add water slowly and knead into a soft dough. Cover and rest for 20 minutes.

3. Make the Veg Filling

Heat a little oil in a pan. Sauté onion, cabbage, and carrots. Add ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook for 3–4 mins and cool the mix.

4. Shape the Momos

Roll out small dough balls into circles. Add a spoonful of filling in the center. Fold and pleat the edges to seal in your preferred shape.

5. Steam and Serve

Place momos in a steamer and steam for 10–12 minutes. Serve hot with spicy momo chutney or soy sauce!
